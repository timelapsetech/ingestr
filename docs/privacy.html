Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026 · Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how Time Lapse Technologies (“Time Lapse Technologies,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you download, install, or use Ingestr, our native application for macOS (the “App”). By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read this Policy.

If you do not agree with this Policy, do not use the App. If you are using the App on behalf of an organization, you represent that you have authority to accept this Policy on its behalf.

2. Scope

This Policy applies solely to the App and information handled in connection with it. It does not apply to third-party websites, services, or applications that we do not control, even if linked from our marketing pages or repository pages.

3. Summary

Ingestr is designed to help you organize and rename image files on your Mac. The App does not collect personal data for us, does not transmit your files or metadata to Time Lapse Technologies, and does not include analytics, advertising, or user tracking. Processing occurs locally on your computer, subject to the permissions you grant through macOS.

4. Information the App processes (locally)

To provide its features, the App may read and write files and metadata that you choose (for example, image files and embedded EXIF or similar metadata such as capture dates) solely on your device in order to copy, rename, verify copies, and organize those files according to your instructions. This processing happens locally and is under your control.

We do not require you to create an account to use the App, and we do not operate sign-in, profiles, or a user database for Ingestr.

5. Information we do not collect from you

Except as stated in this Policy, Time Lapse Technologies does not:

  • Collect, store, or have access to your name, email address, phone number, or similar identifiers through the App;
  • Receive, upload, or synchronize your files or metadata to our servers;
  • Use cookies or similar technologies within the App for tracking;
  • Embed third-party analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs that send data to us or third parties;
  • Use your information for marketing or profiling; or
  • Sell your personal information.

Because we do not receive personal information from the App’s normal operation, there is generally no personal dataset held by us that corresponds to your use of the App.

6. Permissions and system services

macOS may prompt you to grant access to folders, volumes, the Photos library, or related capabilities so the App can read or write the locations you select. Whether and how you grant access is controlled by your operating system settings. We do not receive those permissions or files ourselves—they apply only on your device.

7. Distribution through Apple

If you obtain the App from Apple’s App Store, your use is also subject to Apple’s applicable terms and privacy notices. Apple may process certain information in connection with the App Store (for example, relating to your Apple ID and transactions). We do not control Apple’s practices; see Apple’s privacy documentation for details.

8. Legal bases (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If data protection laws such as the EU or UK General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) apply, and to the extent any processing of personal data occurs on your device in connection with your use of the App, such processing is generally necessary to perform the service you request (organizing and renaming your files locally) and is based on your use of the App and your device settings. Because we do not receive that information, we typically are not in a position to process such personal data on our systems.

9. United States — California privacy rights

California residents may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended (“CCPA”), including rights to know, delete, and opt out of certain processing. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined in the CCPA/CPRA in connection with the App’s operation as described here, and we do not use sensitive personal information for purposes that would require a “Limit the Use of My Sensitive Personal Information” link under the CPRA for this App.

If you contact us and we happen to hold personal information subject to CCPA (for example, an email you voluntarily send to us), we will respond in accordance with applicable law.

10. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App. If you believe we have received such information inadvertently, contact us at the address below and we will take appropriate steps.

11. Security

We design the App to operate locally without sending your content to our infrastructure. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure; you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device, backups, and file permissions.

12. Retention

Because we do not collect personal information through the App’s core operation as described above, we do not retain such information on our systems for those purposes. If you email us, we may retain that correspondence as needed to respond and as required by law.

13. International users

Time Lapse Technologies is based in the United States. If you contact us by email, your message may be processed in the United States or where we operate. Where required, we will describe any cross-border transfers and safeguards in supplemental notices.

14. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version. For material changes, we will provide notice as appropriate (for example, by posting the updated Policy on this page or through release notes). Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy, to the extent permitted by law.
